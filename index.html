Are you Ready to Organize Your Life?

  1. Write your new task into the text field and choose a deadline by using the calendar input. To add the new task to your task list press Enter or click the + button
  2. When you start working on a task, you can click on it to change its status to in-progress (orangered). Clicking it again sets it back to open
  3. Clicking on the checkbox next to a task marks it as done. Yay!
  4. If you want to delete a task from your tasklist permanently, click on the red X
  5. If you want to start fresh and delete your whole tasklist permanently, click the Reset all tasks button

Good to know:

  • Tasks are primarily sorted by their deadline, with the most urgent tasks appearing at the top of the list. When a taskâ€™s status is changed to in-progress, it is moved to the top of its date bracket. Tasks marked as done are automatically moved to the bottom of the entire list
  • All tasks are stored on the website until you manually delete them
